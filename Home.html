our mission
I/O LABS is a provincially incorporated, non-profit organization dedicated to assisting youth in accessing impactful STEM education and opportunities. We raise funds to ensure our programs are always free and focus on authentic engagement with underrepresented communities.
what we do
We are a youth driven organization that aims to foster STEM accessibility, proficiency and engagement through its Advocacy Lab, Education Lab, Events Lab, and Opportunities Lab.
Advocacy Lab
  • We work with community groups to understand and address the barriers faced by underserved populations to STEM educational programming and other opportunities.
  • We initiate projects that focus on improving the accessibility, and the relevance and design of these programs and opportunities.
  • We collaborate with educational institutions on projects that aim to create systemic change.

Education Lab
Free computer programming education for children from low-income households, including workshops, one-on-one mentorship, and coding challenges for prizes.
I/OEd: Python Summer Camp


Students learn Python under the guidance of our experienced and passionate instructors. Basic programming concepts such as Variables, Loops, If-Statements, and Functions are covered in the “Beginner's Room,” while more advanced programming concepts such as Lists and Arrays are taught in the “Advanced Room.” Engagement is further supported through coding challenges, where students apply this knowledge through competition for prizes, with support offered by our weekly tutorials.

I/OEd: Robotics Summer Camp

Six weeks of free robotics education for Indigenous youth, including electronics, robot design and 3D printing, and computer programming. Recognizing the unique needs of Indigenous youth, Robotics Summer Camp runs in tandem with computer programming lessons, allowing participants to experience real world, hands-on applications of what they have learned in coding lessons and delivers much needed relevant programming for this population.

Hackathons and Stuff
I/OHack Jr.


About:

You are invited to I/OHack Jr., a fun all-day coding event that inspires middle school students and introduces them to the world of technology and innovation!

All students in grades 6-9 are welcome, even if you have never written a single line of code! Students from any school may participate in this event. Students may register individually or as a team.

Teams of three or four middle school hackers will collaborate on a software product, with the goal of completing a pitch to present with their team before a judging panel. We will start the day with a lesson, and on-site mentors will be available to support students as they complete their projects.

Our mentors are high school students, with experience teaching summer camp coding classes, leading computer coding club sessions, and managing a competitive robotics programming team. Food will be provided, and there will be awards for the top projects!

*Participants will be given priority registration in our upcoming series of free Python classes.

The importance of Sponsors

Sponsoring us is the best way of supporting us in our endeavours into helping underserved communities through STEM.

Why Should You Sponsor Us?
I/O LABS’s strives to reach a wide number of youth and create a meaningful impact. We have met, worked with, learned from and have been supported by several individuals and organizations throughout our journey. To achieve our goals, we are seeking support, whether that be through financial or non-monetary resources.
We seek to understand our sponsors' missions and appropriately acknowledge sponsors in relevant and creative ways to express your important role in bettering our world.

How Can You Support Us?
There are various ways you can support our organization and its projects and initiatives. Financial sponsorships are critical to achieving our goals and significantly increase the size and quality of the impact we make. Non-monetary sponsorships are also instrumental and can include:
  • event space
  • technology and equipment
  • marketing support
  • food and refreshments
  • mentorship
